Reddit 
is primarily used as a platform for sharing content and participating in conversations about a wide range of topics. Users can ask for advice, share personal experiences, post news stories, or discuss anything from movies to scientific discoveries.
 
The main uses of Reddit, based on user-reported reasons, include keeping up to date with news and current events (31.6%), looking for funny or entertaining content (31.4%), and following or researching brands and products (30.4%).
 It 
is also used for posting or sharing photos or videos (13.1%) and messaging friends and family (7.6%).
 Reddit is often referred to as the "front page of the internet" due to its upvote/downvote system, which helps highlight the most helpful or interesting content.
